Optical illusion is best defined as something that tricks your eyes and makes you think that you can see something that is not there, or makes you see something as different from what it really is.

Three different types of opticla illusions: psychological illusions, literal illusions, and cognitive illusions.

Optical illusions teach us how our eyes and brain work together to see. Many artists use optical illusions to to create phenomena that fool the eye, confusing viewers into seeing more than what is actually there.
